Seafood Chowder mmm

Seafood chowder bits u need:

  • 6 bacon strips
  • 2 celery ribs , finely chopped
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 can of chicken broth
  • 2 cans of seafood chowder
  • 2 bags of seafood
  • a garlic bulb
  • herbs and spices

How to make chowders

  1. mix rib of celery with bacon strips in cup
  2. chop garlic and crush onions into bacon celery mix
  3. boil crab bag in chicken broth
  4. add 2 cans of seafood chowder
  5. allow to cool for minimum of two hours
  6. enjoy cold with sprinkle of herbs and spices
